## Hi there 👋

# Hey, I'm Osaz 👋

Product Design Lead by day, community builder always. I'm based in Lagos, Nigeria, and I spend my time at the intersection of design, technology, and people.

## What I Do

🎨 **Design** — I lead the product design team at [Big Cabal Media](https://bigcabal.com), where we design across [TechCabal](https://techcabal.com), [Zikoko](https://zikoko.com), and other digital media brands reaching millions across Africa. I think in systems — design tokens, component libraries, multi-brand architectures.

🤝 **Community** — I'm a founding member of [Àṣà Coterie](https://twitter.com/asacoterie), a selective multidisciplinary design community I've helped build and lead since 2018. We run education programmes, knowledge-sharing sessions, and IRL events — growing designers across experience levels.

🛠️ **Building** — Co-founding [Kini AI](https://kini-ai.com), an AI upskilling company helping African individuals and organisations build AI skills — and building solutions to match.

## Community & Leadership

Community building is core to how I work. Over the past 7+ years with Àṣà Coterie, I've:

- Designed onboarding systems and managed community operations across 7+ platforms
- Led the Education and Community teams, creating initiatives like **Àṣà Sessions** and **Talking Design With (TDw)**
- Organized recurring events including **Evening Breeze** meetups
- Represented the community at **Google DevFest Lagos**, **Community Managers Fest**, and more
- Built partnerships with aligned organizations to expand reach and shared value
